Top Attractions in Cebu

From majestic waterfalls to historic landmarks, Cebu offers unforgettable adventures for every traveler.

Kawasan Falls

Crystal blue waters, canyoneering adventures, and one of Cebu’s most iconic natural wonders.

Magellan’s Cross

A major historical site marking the beginning of Christianity in the Philippines.

Cebu Taoist Temple

A serene place offering views of the city and intricate Chinese architecture.

Oslob Whale Sharks

Experience swimming with gentle giants up close in the crystal waters of Oslob.

Temple of Leah

Known as the “Taj Mahal of Cebu,” built as a symbol of undying love.

Sinulog Festival

Cebu’s grand cultural festival filled with dancing, colors, and devotion.

Cebu Ocean Park

The largest ocean park in Visayas-Mindanao with immersive marine exhibits.

Osmeña Peak

Cebu’s highest peak known for jagged hills and breathtaking sunrise views.

Casa Gorordo Museum

A preserved Spanish-era home showcasing Cebuano culture and heritage.
