Start Learning Crochet!

Step into the world of crochet with easy beginner tutorials, videos, and tips to help you create your first cozy masterpiece.

Getting Started

Before you start, make sure you have basic materials like yarn, crochet hooks, and scissors. Learn proper grip, tension, and posture to make your experience comfortable and enjoyable.

Beginner Stitches

Chain Stitch (ch)

The foundation of all crochet projects. Learn how to create a chain and use it as a base for other stitches.

Watch Video

Single Crochet (sc)

A simple stitch that is great for beginners. It creates a tight and solid fabric for projects like coasters and amigurumi.

Watch Video

Double Crochet (dc)

A taller stitch that creates a looser texture. Perfect for blankets, scarves, and patterns with more drape.
